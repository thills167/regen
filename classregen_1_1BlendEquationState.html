equationspecifies how source and destination colors are combined. It must be GL_FUNC_ADD,GL_FUNC_SUBTRACT,GL_FUNC_REVERSE_SUBTRACT,GL_MIN,GL_MAX. Initially, both the RGB blend equation and the alpha blend equation are set to GL_FUNC_ADD.

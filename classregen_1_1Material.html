const ref_ptr< ShaderInput1f > & Material::refractionIndex ( ) const

Index of refraction of the material. This is used by some shading models, e.g. Cook-Torrance. The value is the ratio of the speed of light in a vacuum to the speed of light in the material (always >= 1.0 in the real world).
